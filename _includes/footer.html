ARNOVA Workshop on Open Science Tools

Material on this site was presented as part of the 2017 ARNOVA pre-conference workshop designed to introduce participants to useful data science applications and open data resources for nonprofit scholarship.

The workshop was organized by Jesse Lecy and Nathan Grasse in collaboration with the Urban Institute's Center on Nonprofits and Philanthropy and with assistance by Brice McKeever and Francisco Santamarina.
